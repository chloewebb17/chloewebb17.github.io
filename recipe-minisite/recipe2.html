Mayak Eggs

Prep Time: 15 minutes Marinate Time: 24 hours Serving: 6 eggs

Marinated eggs so good they're called "drug eggs" in Korean!

  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil and butter on medium heat in a large, deep pan- use a pot if you don't have one.
  2. Once the oils are hot, place the tomatoes around the edge.
  3. Then place the diced onions, diced bell peppers, and minced garlic in the center.
  4. Cover and cook for at least 10 minutes over medium heat.
  5. In the meantime, slice up some sourdough and brush it with olive oil.
  6. Toast the sourdough slices and set aside for later. This might take a while, depending on the speed of your toaster.
  7. When the tomatoes are soft, mash and stir until the mixture has a saucelike quality.
  8. Mix in the spices and the tomato paste.
  9. Reduce and simmer until any liquid on the top cooks down.
  10. When the sauce is somewhat thick, create 5 wells in the sauce for each egg.
  11. Crack one egg in each well, then cover and cook for 5 minutes or to your liking.
  12. When the eggs are done, remove pan from heat.
  13. Top with fresh parsley.
  14. Serve in the pan, using the toasted sourdough to scoop up heapings of your Shakshuka.
  15. Enjoy!
