Secret Fry Sauce

Prep Time: 1 minute Serving: 1 cup

A recipe I invented as a child that I keep coming back to.

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Smoked Paprika

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Smoked Paprika

Instructions

  1. Scoop three spoonfuls of mayonnaise into your dish
  2. Then two scoops of ketchup
  3. Then one scoop of mustard
  4. Douse throughly in paprika and serve with fries, tater tots, or anything you like!
